3.2.2018

4.00am I dared myself to check on my midterm grades. I'm still to this date relatively afraid of receiving a bad grade. I still think of the future and the repercussions in may bring. However, at this point I've improved how I respond to checking my grades. I'm torn between checking and not checking them. If it didn't really matter, I wouldn't be so scared to check on them. However, deep down inside I know that I'm still fearful and sick and it may be a better idea to just leave it alone. Being more reflective of my thoughts and physical responses, I tried to set the correct mindset first before checking on them. I've had similar experiences before where I wasn't mentally prepared, and the bad grades hit me like a storm and I went dormant for a few days (procrastinated and stuff). I had to be wary this time.

God is always in control. Believe that whatever calamity you think you are experiencing, is actually a blessing in disguise. We simply have to change our perspective and look through God's eyes. Are we sure that what we want is aligned with what God wants?
